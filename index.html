about us.

Discover the story of Loose Biscuit - a band born in the crucible of the '90s alternative scene. From smoky underground clubs to commanding festival stages, our journey is a rollercoaster of passion, rebellion, and unfiltered expression.

Loose Biscuit stands as a testament to the power of nu-metal. Brace yourself for an auditory smack that transcends time, blending heavy beats with soul-shaking melodies. Press play and feel the intensity of nu-metal resurrected.
